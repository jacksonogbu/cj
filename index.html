Our Story

OBODO JACKSON and VICTOR JACKSON met back in primary school. They became best friends after standing up to bullies together. Their friendship strengthened through secondary school, where they faced challenges side-by-side.

After graduation, they gained admission into different universities. Distance entered, but their bond never broke. Calls, visits, shared struggles, and victories kept them together.

Years later, life brought them back into the same city—older, wiser, and still inseparable. Their story continues.
